Tower of Hanoi 3D

Please enter number of rings and press Start.

Instructions

  • Objective: Move all rings from the leftmost peg to the rightmost peg.
  • Rules: Only one ring can be moved at a time, and a larger ring cannot be placed on top of a smaller ring.
  • How to Play: Click and drag a ring to move it to another peg. Use the mouse to rotate the camera view.
  • Controls: Use the buttons above to start a new game, undo moves, or let the solver complete the puzzle automatically.
